About Us

All City Hotdogs is a hotdog stand business founded in 2020 by Tyron Slack in Seattle. Since then, we have served the community with mouthwatering hotdogs with the freshest and highest-quality ingredients.

Our menu offers a range of options, including classic hotdogs with traditional toppings such as ketchup, mustard, and onions, as well as snacks, French fries, and ice-cold sparkling beverages.

At All City Hotdogs, we are committed to providing a delicious and affordable dining option for locals and tourists alike.

Our Mission Statement

We are committed to providing our customers with high-quality food and exceptional service at All City Hotdogs. Our mission is to create a welcoming atmosphere where everyone can enjoy delicious hotdogs made with customer satisfaction in mind.

We strive to be an integral part of the Seattle community by supporting local businesses, participating in community events, and giving back through charitable donations.

All City Hotdogs FAQ

Where do you source your ingredients?

We are committed to using only locally-sourced ingredients to ensure the freshest and highest quality products.

Are your hotdogs gluten-free?

Yes, we offer gluten-free hotdog options for our customers with dietary restrictions.

Do you have any vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we offer vegetarian and vegan hotdog options made with plant-based ingredients.

Can I customize my hotdog order?

Absolutely! We offer a range of toppings and condiments to allow you to personalize your hotdog order to your liking.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Wallet.

Are you open during inclement weather?

Our hotdog stand is open, rain or shine! We have covered seating available for our customers during inclement weather.
